Info

Deets here.

When
If you don't know the date, then you haven't been invited.

Arrive midday Thursday, leave on Sunday when you are sober. Before 4pm.
Where
Fernhill Farm, Somerset BS40 6LD - map

Lifts available from Bristol probably.
What
Music from 11-ish until 2am (HARD STOP), main rig will be indoors with smaller setups outside and in other areas. Fernhill is a festival site and we have a good understanding with the owners.

Fancy dress on Saturday - the theme is "I have a costume!".

Expect all your favourite DJs from last year, but unfortunately, that one you hated is also playing. We couldn't stop them.
Accommodation
There's a large camping field for tents and vans. The field has a toilet block and there's plenty of showers in the main building.

There's also a small number of beds, but they are right next to the main room, so… not peaceful. 2x doubles and 4x singles in bunk layout. Contact Rob or Kate for more info. See: Wren Cottage.

There are other rooms on site for £425 if you really want.
Bring
Booze. Any fun fairy lights/etc. Decorations that you love. Snacks. Breakfast.
Food
There will be NO FOOD provided this year, but we will be organising some group responsibility cooking. There is a BBQ and industrial kitchen. Andy will be organising this.
Rules of Engagement
Treat it like a festival, be discreet, don't make a mess of yourself, or the site. Do NOT shit on the fucking lawn.

Pay up

Hail capitalism.

The cost this year is £180 each.

We appreciate this is late notice, if there is a problem then please get in touch and we'll work something out.
